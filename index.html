We Are Africa's Largest Institute of Deep Technology And Sciences

The Pan African Institute of Technology, Research, and Innovation is a new model for tertiary education which is geared towards raising new generations of African intellects who will drive innovation, propel new industries, and pioneer numerous technological advancements from the African continent.

We seek to improve human capital and brainpower innovation in Africa by providing African disruptors and potential innovators with the right environment, facilities, partnerships and resources that will help them bring new ideas to fruition, ideas that we hope will in time mould Africa into a global innovation and development hub and a launchpad from which a variety of new technological breakthroughs can take off

Courses Sections

Our courses are divided into three sections

Emerging Industries

We train our students in emerging industries so that they can become dominant players when these industries becomes mainstream. In the words of Dr Michio Kaku, "countries that will rise to the top are those who invest in innovative brainpower industries of the future" hence our students receive both practical and theoretical training in these industries of the future. We also equip them with finances and corporate partnerships to enable them carry out research and development, as well as build companies around these industries

Technical Skills

This course section is geared towards training our students to get their hands dirty once in a while. We want our students to be able to make practical use of machines and industrial equipment through the acquisition of technical skills as these are skills which will not only help them in their research and development efforts but also aid their creative process

Auxiliary Fields

This course section involves courses in other fields of study especially those that are outside the realms of science and Technology. Part of what we do is to train our students to become extremely creative and one factor that boosts creativity is the ability to connect a wide variety of ideas from different disciplines. Afterall, you can only think outside the box if you have an idea of what's outside the box
